nest is a nonprofit organization dedicated to empowering artists and artisans around the world.

Nest helps create successful sustainable small businesses by offering a unique combination of interest-free microfinance loans, mentorships, and a market for artisans to sell their crafts. Nest instills pride of ownership, preserves ancient artistic traditions and successfully moves women from poverty to self-sufficiency.
